Tickets


In Kassel, tickets can be purchased at documenta 14 shops or online via the documenta 14 webshop. The ticket grants access to all exhibition venues of documenta 14 in Kassel.

1-day ticket
22 € / reduced* 15 €

2-day ticket
38 € / reduced* 27 €

Season ticket
100 € / reduced* 70 €

Evening ticket
(valid from 5 pm)
10 € / reduced* 7 €

School classes
(per person)
6 €

Family ticket**
50 €

Children (age ten and under)
Free

*Disabled visitors who are eligible to an accompaning person can bring an aide free of charge.

Upon presentation of a valid ID, the following persons are eligible for reduced admission: Students, trainees, recipients of basic social benefits (e.g. ALG II), participants in voluntary service, refugees and severly disabled visitors (from up to 50 percent disability class) receive a discount on tickets upon presentation of the corresponding identification.

**The family ticket is valid for up to two adults with up to three children (up to the age of sixteen).

Directions


From Kassel Airport
Bus 100 or a taxi to Kassel city center.
Taxi at +49 561 881 11, Minicar at +49 561 811 081

From Frankfurt Airport
Direct Deutsche Bahn rail connection (ICE)
to Kassel-Wilhelmshöhe. Information
on timetables and tickets can be found here.

From Hanover Airport
S-Bahn (city and suburban metro/rail service) to Hanover Central Station and from there ICE to Kassel-Wilhelmshöhe. Information on timetables and tickets can be found here.

From Paderborn Lippstadt Airport
Rail connection (regional train) to Kassel-Wilhelmshöhe. Information on timetables and tickets can be found here.
